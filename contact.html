CONTACT US

Have a question, comment, or interested in booking a pottery class? Don't hesitate to get in touch with us at our pottery studio conveniently situated at 2518 S 132nd Ct, Omaha, NE 68144. Our dedicated team is here to assist you with any inquiries you may have. Whether you're looking to learn the art of pottery, book a reservation, or collaborate on a custom piece, we're eager to hear from you. Feel free to reach out via phone at 402-330-3470 or email us at iypcustomerservice@gmail.com. You can also drop by during our studio hours to chat in person. We look forward to connecting with you and sharing our passion for pottery!
