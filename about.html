OUR STORY

It's Yours Pottery is a locally owned Contemporary Paint-Your-Own Pottery Studio, located on the northwest corner of 132nd and Center, Orchard Plaza West. You too can be an artist for a day! Paint pottery, design with glass fusion, hand build with clay, or paint a canvas. We offer a family-friendly creative atmosphere to all who choose to visit! Make time to let your inner artist out!
